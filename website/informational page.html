What is Augmented Reality

Is a direct or indirect live view of a physical, real-world environment whose elements are “augmented” by computer –generated perceptual information, ideally across multiple sensory modalities, including visual, auditory, haptic, somatosensory, and olfactory.

E.g

  • Smartphones
  • Tablets
  • Computers
  • Smart lcd Televisions
